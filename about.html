About this portfolio

I hand-coded this website without the need for artificial intelligence, as I really respect the craft of traditional web design.
Furthermore, I strive not to use as little artificial intelligence as possible in my projects that I showcase on this site and ensure original and authentic pieces of my work.

You see, even though I've spent 8 years as a technologist, I believe truly creative processes heavily rely on human involvement, and I'd like to ensure I assure people of my own personal involvement in my creative works and projects you view on this portfolio website.
